Color Scheme

I have chosen to do a monochromatic color scheme of blues and greys. This look is very trendy and will transcend over the vast demographic that we have. The is gender and age neutral. The blue is very fitting with the name of the website as to blue being associated with water and gray being associated with rain clouds.
