Target Audience

The target of this website is a very large demographic. It is meant for both male and female ages 16-99 in all areas of occupation and all ranges of income. This is the demographic that would have access to a device in order to check the weather conditions to know what to expect to see for the current day.

Personas

Henry Hollingsworth is a construction foreman. He is in charge of a crew of 10 men who do random jobs throughout the Preston area. He takes pride in his work and wants to get his projects done correctly and as quickly as possible to meet all of his client’s goals.

Mattie Metcalf is a stay at home mom with a loving husband and 5 children. She likes to stay involved in her children’s lives, participates in church activities and stays active in her community.

Scenerios

1. Henry needs to know what the weather will be like in order to know if his crew will be able to do their assigned project or if it would be worth it to send them home for the day.

2. Henry project for the day is to pour concrete. Concrete needs to dry for 36 hours before it rains or it will be ruined. Henry needs to know what the weather will be like for the next two days before he can start his job.

3. Mattie has a play date set for today. She needs to know what the weather will be like to know if they are able to go to the park or if they need to do something in doors.

4. Mattie is setting her kids clothes out for school tomorrow. She needs to know what the weather will be like in order for them to dress accordingly.

5. Mattie has helped plan a church activity for Saturday. They want to do an activity outside. She needs to know what the weather will be like to know if that is a possibility or not.
