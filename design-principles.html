Design Principles

Contrast

Lush

https://uk.lush.com/

Lush is a good example of the principle of Contrast because of its contrasting colors. The whole website is Black and White with a loud pop of a bright color. This helps the eye focus on a area that you want to focus on.

Alignment

Google

https://www.google.com

Google is a good website example of the principle of alignment. It has a very strong center alignment. This draws your attention to the center of the page an you instantly know that the search bar is the most important thing on the page and exactly where your eye needs to go to find it.

Proximity

Yahoo

https://www.yahoo.com

Yahoo is a great example of Proximity. They keep all of the similar information together and in similar colors or fonts. This way you know that they are like information and you can tell them apart from the rest of the information on the page.
